It's recommended to use only one H1 tag per page in an HTML document.

Here are some other best practices for H1 tags:

  • Make sure every important page has an H1
  • Include your target keyword
  • Keep H1 tags under 60 characters
  • Make your H1s similar to your title tags
  • Optimize your H1 to satisfy search intent
  • Use one keyword-rich H1 tag: Summarize the whole article with one keyword-rich, confusion-free H1 tag.

Don't use headings to make text BIG or bold.
